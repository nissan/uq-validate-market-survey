This will be a marketing survey application.

The user will be asked a series of questions, and their answers will be used to generate a report.

The user will be able to see the report and share it with others.

The series of questions will be displayed in a series of cards.

The creator of the survey will have a markdown file that will be used to generate the questions and options for answers if the answer is not free form text.

At the end of the survey the user will be asked for their email address and name to receive the report. There should be a checkbox that asks for permission to contact them for follow up.

Create this application using Astro, Tailwind, ShadCN UI and saving the form submission to a Supabase database
